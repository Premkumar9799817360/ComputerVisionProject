Real-time AI proctoring and visual monitoring system. Verify identities, detect spoofing, monitor behavior, and generate intelligent session reports.

🎬 Start Session 📊 Dashboard
🔐

Real-Time Face Verification

Compares live face embeddings with registered identity. Returns match score and confidence level to detect impersonation.

👁

Liveness Detection

Anti-spoofing via frame-to-frame motion analysis and eye detection. Flags photo or video replay attacks instantly.

👥

Multi-Face Detection

Detects when multiple people are in frame. Triggers immediate alert if a second face is detected during session.

🚶

Absence Detection

Monitors candidate presence continuously. Triggers alert if no face detected for more than 5 seconds.

⚠️

Intelligent Risk Scoring

Weighted risk engine aggregates all events into a 0-100 risk score with Safe / Warning / High Risk classification.

🤖

AI Session Summary

LLM generates natural language session summaries after completion. Explainable AI explains why risk score is high.

🧠

Explainable AI

AI explains which events contributed to suspicious behavior and risk score — not just raw numbers.

📄

AI Compliance Report

Downloadable PDF report with risk graphs, event timeline, AI summary, and compliance recommendations.
